How to Enter the Legal Industry in India

  • Complete a 5-year integrated LLB program or a 3-year LLB program after graduation from a recognized university in India.
  • Pass the All India Bar Examination (AIBE) to practice law in India after obtaining your law degree.
  • Gain hands-on experience by interning with law firms, courts, or public interest organizations during your studies.
  • Consider specializing in specific areas such as Corporate Law, Criminal Law, Taxation Law, or Intellectual Property.
  • Apply for judicial services exams to become a judge or join government bodies.
  • Join bar associations and legal forums to network and keep up with the latest legal developments in India.
  • Look for opportunities in Indian law firms, corporate legal departments, NGOs, and the government.
